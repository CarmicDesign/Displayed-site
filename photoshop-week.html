Project Brief

Develop one digital image per week to an assigned theme. These are subjective themes that are open for exploration and can be interpreted many different ways. Try to produce a mix of techniques and explore a range of imaging styles - breakout of your comfort zone and experiment. Use your cameras, draw, scan or whatever... get creative and be original! Explore photo media and imaging techniques!
‍
Following are each week's theme:

  1. Solar powered future
  2. Golden hour
  3. Weird science
  4. Colour theory
  5. World within world

Art does not exist only to entertain, but also to challenge one to think, to provoke, even to disturb, in a constant search for truth.
