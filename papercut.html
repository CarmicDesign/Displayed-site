Rise and Shine

Day scene illustration with paper-cut effect

This illustration portrays the rich culture and environment of country Bhutan with some of the traditional style of clouds, stupa and tree. I tried to blend the traditional motifs with contemporary design which was successfully achieved through paper cut out effect with choice of landscape colour theme. 
This minimal vector is also my simple gesture of early morning view of landscape in those mix art in digital form.
