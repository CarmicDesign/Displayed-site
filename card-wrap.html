Project Brief

PART 1Choose one of the following topics:
- Canberra Light Rail Canberra
- Balloon Spectacular Canberra
- Short Film Festival
‍
Conduct some initial research on your chosen topic, you task here is to understand what the organisation is about by defining what it represents or its main function. Think about what it means to you, as this understanding will help refine the image selection and drawings.
‍
PART 2Collect multiple images that relate to one of the three topics above that represent a range of different photographs, drawings, graphics or symbols. Include any images from around the world that relate to this topic.
‍
PART 3Generate minimum of three concept sketches that can be drawn in illustrator to be used for survey which can be then refined to design a final piece.
‍
PART 4Find a range of people who don’t know what you’re working on to gauge which of the three concepts has the clearest communication. Remember that the ideal response will be a close match to the words you have written in your definition.
‍
PART 5Produce a single refined symbol design based on the responses from the survey. The Final Symbol/Logo can now have the required colour applied and must be drawn in Adobe Illustrator.
‍
PART 6Create a packaging design that uses the logo brand and additional type information to create a realistic promotional product.
‍
Collect all the information required for the pack then design the pack with a clear focus on colour, typographic hierarchy, care in font selection, tracking, kerning and leading as well as information about what the pack is and it's contents. Design the pack with the aim of mass producing it in Pantone Process Colour (CMYK). The final illustrator file must also include bleed, die cuts and any folds and be actual size or 100%.


Research

Canberra Light Rail

Serving the people in the city of Canberra. 12 km light rail links City-Gungahlin-City with 13 stops in between.
Its goal is to reduce traffic congestion with increase in population and offering commuters with wide range of travel options.

Mood board

Study on collection of logos and symbols

Most of the logo/symbol uses only two colours to contrast the image more clear and simple but the shape, styles, size, colour, perspective are used differently.

Most of the symbols that i collected are without type and those are very effective to communicate the message clearly.

Almost all the symbols collected seems to be designed after 19th Century since its very simple and contemporary, unlike the past where details are much of the interest.

I will be mainly working with black and white colour which would be easier to adapt with colour later on for flexibility. The kind of style would be outline with minimal look and will try to work with simple shape mostly.
